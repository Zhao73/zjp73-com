NumberFall

Roguelike Number Puzzle x RPG

About

NumberFall is a unique roguelike number puzzle game that blends 2048-style mechanics with RPG character progression. Choose your character, unlock traits, and descend through procedurally generated floors of number challenges.


Features

  • Roguelike progression with floor-based difficulty
  • 6 unique characters with distinct abilities
  • 110+ traits with quality tiers and synergies
  • Trait Codex encyclopedia for collectors
  • Pixel-perfect retro aesthetic
