Shiftru

Shift Management & Salary Calculator

About

Shiftru is the ultimate shift management app for part-time workers in Japan. Easily manage your work schedule, automatically calculate your salary, and stay aware of the 1.03 million yen tax threshold. Features Live Activities on your lock screen, Siri Shortcuts, and calendar sync.


Features

  • Shift scheduling with calendar sync
  • Automatic salary calculation
  • 103-man yen threshold tracking
  • Live Activities & Dynamic Island
  • Siri Shortcuts integration
  • Multiple theme & app icon options
  • iCloud data sync
