Kakeibo

Smart Household Expense Tracker

About

Kakeibo is a personal finance management app inspired by the traditional Japanese household budgeting method. Track your daily expenses, scan receipts with your camera, and sync everything across your devices with iCloud.


Features

  • Camera-based receipt scanning
  • iCloud sync across all your devices
  • Home screen widget for quick expense view
  • Category-based expense tracking
  • Monthly budget reports and insights
