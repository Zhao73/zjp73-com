Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

ZJP ("we", "our", "us") operates the Kakeibo mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Kakeibo is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Expense data is stored locally and in your private iCloud account
  • Receipt images are processed on-device only
  • Anonymous usage analytics (crash reports)
  • No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties

3. Camera Usage

Kakeibo uses your camera solely for receipt scanning. Images are processed on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

4. iCloud Sync

Your expense data syncs via Apple's iCloud service. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible by us.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store (distribution and in-app purchases)
  • Apple iCloud (data synchronization)
  • Google AdMob (advertisements in free version)

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
