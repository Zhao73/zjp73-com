Privacy Policy - Breeder Note

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

ZJP ("we", "our", "us") operates the Breeder Note mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Kennel and dog profile data (stored locally and in Firebase/iCloud)
  • Voice recordings for transcription (processed via AI, not stored permanently)
  • Staff scheduling and operational data
  • Anonymous usage analytics and crash reports

3. Voice Data

Voice-to-record features use AI transcription. Audio is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers after transcription is complete.

4. Data Storage

  • Data is stored locally on your device with offline-first architecture
  • Cloud sync via Firebase and/or iCloud when connected
  • Multi-tenant data is isolated per kennel organization

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication, database, storage)
  • Google AI (voice transcription)
  • Apple iCloud (backup)

6. Data Security

We implement role-based access control. Admin and employee accounts have separate permission levels. All data in transit is encrypted.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for professional kennel operators. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries: zjp@zjp73.com
