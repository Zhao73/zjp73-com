Privacy Policy - Shiftru

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Shiftru handles the following information:

  • User-entered data (workplace name, hourly wage, shifts, break times, salary settings)
  • App settings (notification preferences, theme settings)
  • Information for ad delivery & analytics (advertising ID, device info, usage data, diagnostics)

2. How We Use Information

  • Providing shift management, salary calculation, notifications, and display features
  • Bug fixes, quality improvement, and usage analytics
  • Displaying and optimizing ads in the free plan

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (ad delivery) - policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple (StoreKit / iCloud platform features)

4. Tracking (ATT) & Ad Consent

Shiftru may request permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT). You can change your tracking preferences at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is primarily stored on your device
  • If enabled, backup data may be stored in iCloud Drive
  • We implement reasonable security measures but cannot guarantee absolute security

6. Your Choices

  • Ad-related consent can be changed in device settings
  • In-app data can be edited, deleted, or removed by uninstalling the app
  • Deletion requests can be sent via the contact below

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy in response to legal changes or service updates. Significant changes will be announced on this page.

9. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries: support@shiftru.app
